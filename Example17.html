sumOfSumOfDigits - LNCT Bhopal Ravi is trying to develop a sum function which have sum of 1-digit no formed from given input1, sum of 2-digits no formed from given input1, sum of 3-digits no formed from given input1, sum of 4-digits no formed from given input1, and so on till n-digits, where n is the length of input1. Number formation is done on the given condition: 1-digit numbers are formed by using all digits from number individually. 2-digits numbers are formed by using first and second digit of input, then second and third digit and so on till n-1 digit.
